Fitness of the Body/

So often, we move through the world unaware of our bodies' natural potential. In yoga, we target specific muscle groups for strength and flexibility. The intentional sequencing and flow of poses allows you to deeply engage with the process and unlock your body's full potential.

Fitness of the Mind/

Keeping your mind just as healthy as your body is made simpler when yoga is part of your fitness routine. Pairing breath with movement and reconnecting to the self significantly improves mood and memory, and reduces stress and anxiety. Reach out to me to set up your first class today!

ANNA'S APPROACH/

"In my experience, some studios can be an intimidating and competitive space to practice, which runs contrary to the meaning of yoga. My hope is to reconnect the practice of yoga, meaning to yolk or bring together, to its roots and to provide a supportive and comfortable environment in which students and instructors can learn from each other. When I first became ceritified, I searched for a community of people who fostered each other's growth rather than a search for the perfect photo. Eventually, I created my own community of yogis filled with supportive students and instructors who motivate each other, and it continues to grow. Before our first class, you and I will take the time to discuss your spiritual, fitness, and mindfullness goals so that I can design a personalized routine for you or your group. Whether it's your first class or your 20th year practicing I would love to hear from you and welcome you into my community."

-Anna Stella
